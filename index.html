Welcome

This web site is designed for the Backflow Prevention Assembly certification data. Test results from annual inspections are recorded to this site.

This application is for authorized users only. Contractors must be registered with the Cross Connection Control Office and possess a valid, up-to-date plumbing license, current insurance, backflow test/repair certification and calibration of equipment before access will be granted.
